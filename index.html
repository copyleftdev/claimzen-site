How it works

Three steps. Nothing sent without you.

01

Reads the denial

Every payer message — email, EOB, even the PDF — is parsed and correlated to the claim it concerns. Nothing gets lost in the inbox.

02

Checks your records

Prior auth, timely filing, duplicates, underpayments, patient-responsibility errors, invalid CARC codes — eight checks against your own practice records and the clearinghouse.

03

Drafts the appeal

When the payer is wrong, an evidence-backed appeal is already written — citing the claim, the code, and the record that refutes it. You review, and one click sends it.
