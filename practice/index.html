The intelligence

Four coprocessors. Every one proposes; you sign.

The model does the reading, coding, and drafting. It never makes a fact. Each proposal passes a deterministic gate — grounded in the record — and a licensed human signs it into a signed event.

Ambient scribe

Turns the operatory transcript into a structured clinical note and coded procedures — every procedure grounded in a verbatim phrase from the transcript.

◈ clinician approves → signed chart

Imaging read

Reads a radiograph into tooth-level findings — caries, bone loss — each on a valid tooth and surface. A second read, never an auto-diagnosis.

◈ clinician is the read of record

Chart-to-claim

Assembles a claim from the charted facts — coding only what was charted, with the charge equal to the summed fees. A claim that defends itself.

◈ biller approves → claim submitted

Appeal drafting

When a payer denies wrongly, drafts the appeal citing the claim, the denial code, and the record that refutes it. ClaimZen recovery, built in.

◈ signed evidence per case
